About Me

Tinashe Giyavha is a data engineer at the UC Berkeley extension’s Marketing department. Before that, he was a program manager with UC extension’s new ventures where he helped introduce new courses online. In his current position, he is building out the data infrastructure to support the data driven marketing team. Prior to joining Extension, he earned a Masters in Industrial Engineering and Operations from UC Berkeley. Tinashe’s interest is in tech for social good. A native of Zimbabwe, he is currently working on online social media platform that can keep Zimbabwean parliamentary members accountable for their campaign promises. He is avid volleyball player and enjoys hiking around the Bay Area. Ask him anything about small scale gold mining in Zimbabwe!
